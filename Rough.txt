App Name: Echoes
Subtitle: Lost stories, around you.

Core Functionality:
- Allow users to post stories (title, body, hashtags, optional images)
- Allow users to view stories based on the location he is in.
- Allow users to view stories that his friends/followers have posted.
- Allow users to view stories from locations in any place of the world (premium feature). //later.
- Social integration - Twitter/Facebook Sign Up - Social sharing of stories with friends.

v1.0
----
- USBAT post stories and attach it to the location that he is currently in.
- USBAT view stories in the location that he is currently in. Nearby stories will be retrieved based on Friends - Popular - Latest (tabbed).
- USBAT like stories and share in social media.
